 Why a website?

In the age of the Internet - a resource so widely used whether you're stationary or mobile - we believe that one of the biggest and crucial steps in expanding a business would be to establish an online presence so readily accessible by anyone anywhere in the globe. Promoting portfolios, products and services couldn't get any easier than sharing your business address - a URL.

Investing in a website may seem costly especially when you're just starting, but we believe the loss would eventually be greater if you've chosen not to and will outweigh how much you think you've saved, most especially when competitors in the market have already taken the lead.

How long does development usually take?

A project's duration varies according to scope and complexity taking around a month at a minimum. This is to allocate enough time for optimization and testing, as we aim to deliver quality solutions with the least number of issues as possible. However, less complex applications i.e. sites with up to 3 static pages can sometimes be done in only 2-3 weeks.

Urgent projects will require more people in the project and shall be billed at an extra rate, which shall be disclosed to you prior to onboarding.

What is a minimum viable product?

An MVP is a product version with a bare minimum set of features, just enough to solicit feedback from customers in the early stages of a product. Most business owners often prefer these rather than paying for full-featured systems in one go to save from investing on unnecessary work which, at the end, may or may not be positively received by their end-users.

Our development rates have been structured in favor of producing MVPs unless, otherwise, stated and/or requested by the client.

Will I need post-development maintenance for my application?

Yes, as all applications would. Over time, with the rapid updates in technology, software libraries and design trends along with evolving customer needs, every application will need to keep up. Otherwise, it would eventually look and feel outdated or worse, degrade in performance over time.

Do you do post-development maintenance?

The amount that's been billed to you by this time was for the development of the application's MVP. We offer 3 months of post-development maintenance free of charge. During such time, we shall no longer be doing any additional feature implementation and/or changes from what was originally agreed on.

Though we'd still be happy to help you further - take care of any maintenance work and/or add any awesome features, but it shall be considered a new project and shall be charged separately at discounted rates.

If you do not wish for us to do so, we want to help you with the transition so we shall be providing you all project-related credentials along with a technical documentation which could be used by the team who will be maintaining your application moving forward.
