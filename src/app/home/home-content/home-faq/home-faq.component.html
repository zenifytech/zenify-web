What is an MVP application?

An minimum viable product or MVP is a product version with a bare minimum set of features, just enough to solicit feedback from customers in the early stages of a product. Most business owners often prefer these rather than paying for full-featured systems in one go to save from investing on unnecessary work which, at the end, may or may not be positively received by their end-users.

Any quotation we provide is usually for building MVP applications unless, otherwise, stated and/or requested by the client. Full-featured applications will differ significantly in cost and will be billed monthly.

Do I need post-development maintenance for my application?

Yes, as all applications would. Over time, with the rapid updates in technology, software libraries and design trends along with evolving customer needs, every application will need to keep up. Otherwise, it would eventually look and feel outdated or worse, degrade in performance over time.

Do you do post-development maintenance?

The amount that's been billed to you by this time was for the development of the application's MVP. We offer 3 months of post-development maintenance free of charge. During such time, we shall no longer be doing any additional feature implementation and/or changes from what was originally agreed on.

Though we'd still be happy to help you further - work on new and awesome features on top of maintenance, but it shall be considered a new project and shall be charged separately at discounted rates.

If you do not wish for us to do so, we want to help you with the transition so we shall be providing you all project-related credentials along with a technical documentation which could be used by the team who will be maintaining your application moving forward.
