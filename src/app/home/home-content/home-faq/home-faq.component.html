What is a minimum viable product?

An MVP is a product version with just enough set of features in the system to receive customer feedback in its early stages or to jumpstart businesses as soon as possible. Most business owners often prefer to go for these instead of paying for full-featured systems in one go to save from investing on unnecessary work which, at the end, may or may not be positively received by customers.

Our development rates have been structured in favor of producing MVPs unless, otherwise, stated and/or requested by the client.

Yes, as all applications would. Over time, with the rapid updates in technology, software libraries and design trends along with evolving customer needs, every application will need to adapt to these changes. Otherwise, it would eventually look and feel outdated and/or perform poorly.
